Visitor Center 2011/2016

This was a work of passion. One of my favorite movies is “Jurassic Park.” This is reflected in my work. I went to great lengths to make this as screen accurate as possible. I modeled this a few years ago in Rhino 4.0 and updated and animated it in 3ds Max.

• Modeled in Rhino 4.0 (Updated in 3ds Max)
• Rendered with Mental Ray

Pixar Studios Lobby 2015

This was a solo project I worked on for a couple months. I based all my work on a few reference photos of the location. Since the angles of the reference photos didn’t allow me to see everything I decided to improvise and make it only reminiscent of the real location. I did this by eliminating a doorway, some miscellaneous features and recreating the paintings as vague representations.

• Modeled in 3ds MAX 2015
• Used a global lighting system along with Mental Ray light portals
